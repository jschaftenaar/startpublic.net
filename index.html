Discover why open communication is more effective than private messages.

Learn More

This idea is inspired by NoHello.net, but our focus is on the positive aspects of starting communication in public channels rather than the negative. Private messaging has its place, especially for confidential information. For help questions, start public—it’s okay to tag people publicly. By embracing open communication from the start, there's no need for NoHello.

Why Choose Public Channels?

Transparency: Public discussions ensure that everyone has access to the same information.

Inclusivity: Involving multiple people can generate diverse perspectives and solutions.

Efficiency: Avoids repetitive questions and answers, as discussions are available to all.

Benefits of Public Communication

  • Open Knowledge Sharing: All participants can benefit from shared insights and solutions.
  • Enhanced Collaboration: Easy to track progress and contributions.
  • Reduced Misunderstandings: Clearer context and more robust documentation of discussions.
